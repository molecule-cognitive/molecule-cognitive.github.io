Core Project

McE — the Cognitive Engine

McE ingests knowledge from research papers, academic databases, and web sources — but instead of treating information as unstructured text, it converts everything into structured entities and relationships within a growing knowledge graph.



Every paper, every concept, every citation becomes a node. Every relationship a traversable edge. The graph grows with each ingestion cycle.

Long-Term Vision

Persistent machine cognition.

The long-term goal is systems that don't just retrieve — they think. Continuously acquiring knowledge, organizing it into structured networks, and enabling reasoning over information at a scale no human team could sustain.



McE is the first layer. The infrastructure that makes everything above it possible.
